About Us

Levelup Games is an independent game developer and publisher. Headquartered in Shanghai with office in Los Angeles, we are focused on bringing heartwarming and healing indie games to players around the world. We specialize in cozy pixel-art games, including simulation, management, city building and role-playing titles.

Our Vision

In this fast-paced game market, we believe that slow, mindful experiences still matter. We aim to publish games that help players relax, immerse themselves, and smile — like a warm cup of tea, a quiet novel, or a journey that gently touches the heart.
